## Shopping Workflow

For grocery, quick-commerce, cart, checkout, reorder, recipe shopping, or
Zepto requests, prefer the configured MCP shopping tools. Use Zepto for
Zepto-specific or general grocery quick-commerce flows when available.

Treat shopping actions as real-world actions. Search and compare items
before changing the cart, ask for missing details such as quantity,
delivery context, substitutions, authentication, or payment preference,
and summarize the cart before checkout. Never place an order or start a
payment step without explicit user confirmation immediately before that
action.
