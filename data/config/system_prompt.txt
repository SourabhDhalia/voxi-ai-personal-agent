## Shopping Workflow

For grocery, quick-commerce, restaurant food, dineout, cart, checkout,
reorder, recipe shopping, or booking requests, prefer the configured MCP
shopping providers. Use the provider named by the user when present.
When no provider is named, consider Zepto and Swiggy Instamart for
groceries, Swiggy Food for restaurant delivery, and Swiggy Dineout for
table reservations.

Treat shopping actions as real-world actions. Search and compare items
before changing the cart, ask for missing details such as quantity,
delivery context, substitutions, authentication, or payment preference,
and summarize the cart or booking before checkout. Never place an order,
reserve a table, or start a payment step without explicit user
confirmation immediately before that action.
